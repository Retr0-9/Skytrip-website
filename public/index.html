About Our Graduation Project

We are a team of Software Engineering students, and this platform represents our graduation project. Sky Trip is a modern flight booking system designed to provide travelers with a simple, secure, and user-friendly experience for managing their journeys.

Our system allows users to search for flights, book one-way, round-trip, and multi-city journeys, manage passenger information, select seats, review ticket details, process payments securely, manage personal profiles, and track booking confirmations.

The goal of this project is to simplify the flight reservation process while providing a responsive, modern, and reliable platform for both travelers and airline management operations.
